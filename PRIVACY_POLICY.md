# Privacy Policy for Order Delivery Exporter

**Last Updated: December 7, 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Order Delivery Exporter ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Shopify application.

## Information We Collect

### Data from Shopify
When you install our App, we access the following data from your Shopify store:

- **Order Information**: Order numbers, dates, status, totals
- **Customer Information**: Names, email addresses, phone numbers (for export purposes only)
- **Shipping Information**: Shipping and billing addresses
- **Fulfillment Information**: Fulfillment status, tracking numbers, delivery dates
- **Line Items**: Product names, SKUs, quantities, prices

### Session Data
We store minimal session data to maintain your authentication with Shopify:
- Shop domain
- Access tokens (encrypted)
- User ID and basic profile information

## How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data solely for the following purposes:

1. **Order Export**: To generate Excel/CSV files containing your order and delivery information
2. **Authentication**: To verify your identity and maintain secure access to your store
3. **App Functionality**: To display order information within the app interface

**We do NOT:**
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your data for marketing purposes
- Share your data with anyone except as required by law
- Store customer data permanently (only session data is retained)

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage
- **Session Data**: Stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway.app
- **Customer/Order Data**: NOT stored permanently; only accessed in real-time when you use the app
- **Exported Files**: Generated on-demand and downloaded directly to your device

### Security Measures
- All data transmission uses HTTPS/SSL encryption
- Database connections are encrypted
- Access tokens are stored securely
- Only authenticated users can access the app
- Regular security updates and monitoring

## Data Retention

- **Session Data**: Retained for the duration of your app installation
- **Customer/Order Data**: NOT retained; accessed only when you actively use the app
- **Exported Files**: Stored only on your local device; we do not keep copies

When you uninstall the app, all session data is automatically deleted.

## Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except:

1. **Shopify**: As required for app functionality
2. **Railway.app**: Our hosting provider (covered by their privacy policy)
3. **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect our rights

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the data we store about your shop
- Request deletion of your data (by uninstalling the app)
- Opt-out of data collection (by not using the app)
- Request a copy of your data

## Customer Data Protection

We respect the privacy of your customers:

- Customer data is only accessed when you explicitly export orders
- We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection regulations
- Customers' consent decisions are respected
- We do not use customer data for any purpose other than order export

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

- **Shopify**: For app hosting and API access
- **Railway.app**: For application hosting and database services

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their use of data.
